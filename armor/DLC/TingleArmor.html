To get the "Tingle's Armor"-Set, you'll need

  • To look for the Book in the Ruins, north of The Great Plateau.
  • The Hood:
    1. Head South of the Central Tower and find the "Exchange Ruins"
    2. Once there, go between the bodies of water, there should be an "EX Chest"

  • The Shirt:
    1. Just west of Hyrule Castle is an Island, "Castle Town Prison"
    2. On the East coast is a Guardian protecting the Treasure.
    3. Sometimes bird stand around the Chest.

  • The Pants:
    1. Head East from the Central Tower, you'll see two roads meet.
    2. From that point run North to "Mabe Village Ruins"
    3. You'll see a dead Guardian in a pool, just under it will be the Chest.
