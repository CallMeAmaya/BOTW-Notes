To get the "Phantom Armor"-Set you'll need

  • To look for the Book in the Ruins, north of The Great Plateau
  • The Helmet:
    1. You'll find the Helmet on the bottom floor of the Coliseum
    2. If you enter from the back, it's behind a pillar on the right side
  • The Chestplate:
    1. Go to the Central Tower and run towards the Sacred Grounds Ruins
    2. The Chest is right at the first Memory next to two broken Pillars
  • The Greaves:
    1. Go to the Central Tower, the Greaves are next to a Guardian outside the closest Ruins
    2. Just keep walking South-East inside the ruins, you'll find a broken down Guardian
